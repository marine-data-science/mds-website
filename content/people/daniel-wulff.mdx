### Junior Research Group Medical Image Computing

Our *Junior Research Group Medical Image Computing* focuses on advancing modern 
machine learning methods for medical imaging for tasks such as segmentation, detection and tracking. 
Building on the research areas outlined above, we are especially interested in 
developing reliable and interpretable models that can support decision-making 
in real-world clinical settings.

We are always looking for motivated students interested in Bachelor's or Master's theses, 
as well as collaborators from academia and industry. 
If you are interested in working with us or exploring related topics, feel free to reach out.

More information about ongoing [projects](/projects/) and available opportunities can be found 
in [theses](/theses/) or on our dedicated group website: [Junior Research Group Medical Image Computing](https://wulle192.github.io/MICgroup/).
