# 08 - Final Review And Demo Close

## Purpose

Use this near the end of the recorded workflow. The goal is to produce a publish-readiness review and a concise narrative for the workshop.

This prompt turns the final state back into teaching material.

## Copy-paste prompt

```text
Please perform a final review of the demo website and prepare a concise handoff summary.

Review:
- What works well?
- What still needs human review before publishing?
- Are there content issues, missing metadata, missing images, or uncertain claims?
- Are there accessibility, responsiveness, or deployment risks?
- Did we preserve source provenance?

Also produce a short workshop-demo summary with:
- The main agent workflow steps we went through.
- The most important human decision points.
- One example of where context improved the result.
- One example of where review/redirect was necessary.
- A concise closing message for the audience.

Do not make large code changes unless you find a small critical fix. If you do make a fix, verify it.
```

## Review checkpoint

This is the final teaching frame:

- What did the agent do?
- What did the human still have to decide?
- What became easier?
- What became more important to review?

## Good outcome

A final summary exists that can be used for narration, documentation, or a later blog post.
